Assessment & Evaluation

Modern Student Evaluation System

Our assessment framework focuses on academic excellence, conceptual understanding, practical learning, and continuous student development through structured evaluation methods and performance tracking.

The assessment framework at Wisdom Park International School is designed to track continuous progress and support the holistic development of students. Following CBSE guidelines, the school adopts a Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation (CCE) approach that considers both academic and co-curricular performance.

Key Features

  • Ongoing assessment throughout the academic year
  • Blend of oral, written, and practical evaluations
  • Emphasis on conceptual understanding and application
  • Regular and constructive feedback for students and parents

The school follows a well-structured examination process to measure academic performance and track student progress effectively. Assessments are conducted at regular intervals to ensure consistent improvement.

Assessment Components

  • Periodic Tests
  • Term Examinations (Half-Yearly & Annual Examination)
  • Internal Assessments (Notebooks, Assignments, Activities)
  • Practical and Project-Based Evaluations

The school follows the CBSE grading system to ensure fair and transparent evaluation. Grades are awarded based on the marks obtained, promoting a balanced and student-friendly assessment process.

Academic Performance Criteria

Academic Excellence & Evaluation Hub

The school ensures that students achieve academic excellence through a balanced, transparent, and comprehensive assessment system designed to support continuous growth and learning. The evaluation process focuses on both scholastic and co-scholastic development while encouraging conceptual understanding, creativity, and practical application of knowledge in daily learning activities.

Evaluation criteria include performance in written examinations, periodic tests, assignments, project work, practical activities, attendance, classroom participation, and overall academic progress. The school follows CBSE guidelines and minimum passing requirements to ensure students maintain consistent performance, discipline, and active involvement throughout the academic year.
